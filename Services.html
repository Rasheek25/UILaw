SERVICES

Property Division

Divorce can be a highly emotional experience, and the division of marital assets and debts can further complicate the process. At UILaw, we understand the importance of a fair and equitable outcome, one that reflects your contributions to the marriage. Our experienced attorneys will work diligently to ensure you receive a just settlement. Here's what we can offer:

  • Detailed Asset Valuation: We will meticulously identify and value all marital assets and debts, including real estate, investments, retirement accounts, vehicles, and personal belongings.
  • Skilled Negotiation: Our attorneys are adept negotiators who will advocate for your interests in obtaining a fair share of marital property. We will leverage our knowledge of family law and financial analysis to build a strong case on your behalf.
  • Litigation Expertise: While we always strive for an amicable resolution, we are prepared to represent you effectively in court if necessary. Our lawyers have extensive experience in property division litigation and will fight to protect your financial rights.

We understand that navigating property division during divorce can be stressful. UILaw will provide clear communication and keep you informed throughout the process, ensuring you have a full understanding of your options and the progress of your case.

Divorce

UILaw recognizes that divorce is a challenging and emotional experience for all parties involved. We are committed to providing you with the legal guidance and support you need to navigate this difficult time. Here's how we can help:

  • Comprehensive Legal Advice: Our attorneys will explain your legal rights and options concerning child custody, spousal support, alimony, and property division. We will address your specific concerns and guide you towards the most favorable outcome for your situation.
  • Custody and Support Negotiations: We are skilled negotiators who can work towards a parenting plan and a fair and sustainable support agreement that prioritizes the well-being of your children.
  • Trial Advocacy: If necessary, our experienced attorneys will represent you effectively in court, advocating for your rights and interests throughout the divorce proceedings.

UILaw understands the emotional toll divorce can take. We are committed to providing compassionate and understanding support throughout the process.

Family Agreements

Planning for the future of your family promotes clarity and security. At UILaw, we understand the importance of having clear legal agreements in place to protect your interests in different scenarios. We can draft and review various family-related contracts, including:

  • Prenuptial Agreements (Prenups): A prenuptial agreement outlines how assets and debts will be divided in the event of divorce. This can be especially beneficial if one partner is entering the marriage with significant assets or debts.
  • Cohabitation Agreements (Cohabitation Pacts): These agreements define financial responsibilities and ownership of property for unmarried couples living together.
  • Postnuptial Agreements: A postnuptial agreement allows spouses to modify their existing financial arrangements after marriage.

By having these agreements in place, you can avoid potential conflicts and ensure your wishes are respected in the event of separation or unforeseen circumstances. UILaw will work closely with you to ensure the agreement reflects your specific needs and protects your financial future.
